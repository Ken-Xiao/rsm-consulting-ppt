WHERE THE BEAT CAME FROM
By segment: Data Cloud (+86% cc) is the marginal driver — five quarters now of triple-digit ARR growth. Sales/Service Cloud are the cash engines (cc +11.2% / +8.4%), still well above peer average.

By geo: Americas LE is the unmistakable signal — 84 seven-figure deals vs. 62 prior year. EMEA constructive even excluding FX; APAC ex-Japan remains the soft spot.

Marketing Cloud: the only soft pocket — three large renewals slipped from Q3 to Q4 (Q3 vs cons −1.6%). Slide 07 covers the read-through; not a structural concern.
