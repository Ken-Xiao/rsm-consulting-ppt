15  ·  Risks & How We Manage Them
The three things you are worried about — pre-empted.
Distilled from the CFO conversation on 6 May, your written RFP feedback, and three procurement-side questions that surfaced before this session.
01
Your concern
"Customer churn risk if we re-price 11 contracts in one quarter."
Top accounts are sensitive. A heavy-handed re-pricing wave triggers competitor poaching, exactly in the IPO window.
How we manage it
Contract-by-contract redesign, not list-price uplift. ChemCorp lost zero of its top 20 accounts.
Index-linkage and term changes recover margin without a headline price increase. We coach the first three deal conversations in-room before scale.
02
Your concern
"Partner attention will drop after week 4."
Procurement has been told this twice in prior engagements; partner pulled by week 6, manager left running the engagement.
How we manage it
Lead Partner attendance contractual. 40% time commitment in the SOW, with fee remedy if missed.
Two Tuesdays missed in any four-week window triggers a 10% fee credit. Six named individuals; substitutions require sponsor approval.
03
Your concern
"Findings the IPO bankers won't underwrite."
Aspirational numbers that fall apart in due diligence. Atlas's prior advisor produced a model that didn't survive the second roadshow day.
How we manage it
Bottom-up 24-account model, audit-defensible attribution at G4.
Every recovered-margin dollar tied to a named contract and a dated counter-signature. Atlas CFO will speak to this directly on a reference call.
