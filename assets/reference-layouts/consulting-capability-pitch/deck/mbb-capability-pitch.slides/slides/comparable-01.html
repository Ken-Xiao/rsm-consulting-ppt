06  ·  Comparable Engagement 01  ·  ChemCorp EMEA
Renewal-pricing transformation delivered +$180M EBITDA in four quarters at 22% IRR.
Situation
A $2.4B EMEA coatings & specialty-resins business losing 380 bps of EBITDA margin in three quarters.
Input-cost shock had passed through to spot pricing but not to multi-year renewal contracts. Sales leadership owned new wins; nobody formally owned renewal economics. CFO had three quarters before a board-mandated review.
Our Work
A 16-week, six-person engagement organized around 47 renewal contracts rolling in the next 90-day window.
Built a contract-by-contract redesign playbook (index-linkage, volume-tier rebuild, take-or-pay reset). Stood up a weekly SteerCo with the CCO and CFO; coached the deal team through the first eight conversations directly.
Outcome
+$180M EBITDA in four quarters. 22% IRR. Zero customer losses on the top 20 accounts.
Margin recovered 260 bps within two quarters. Renewal-pricing function permanently transferred to the regional CCO. Playbook is the working asset we propose to redeploy for Halsey (see slide 10).
