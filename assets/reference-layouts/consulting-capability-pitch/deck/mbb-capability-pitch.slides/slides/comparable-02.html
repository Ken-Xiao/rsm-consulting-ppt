07  ·  Comparable Engagement 02  ·  Sentral Polymers
Tier-2 coverage rebuild lifted win-rates 12 points across 19 metros in eight months.
Situation
A $1.6B polymers business with single-source distributor coverage in 24 second-tier cities post-consolidation.
Win-rates had fallen 14 points year-over-year in exactly the geographies where regional competitors were hiring. Three quarters of leadership debate had not produced a decision between distributor renegotiation and direct-sales rebuild.
Our Work
Twelve-week diagnostic produced a city-by-city play: re-cut distributor terms in 14 cities, direct-sales rebuild in 5.
Pulled customer-by-customer win/loss data the client had collected but never analyzed. Built the recruiting profile and ride-along program for the 42 net new reps. Stayed engaged through hire 30 to prove the model before stepping off.
Outcome
+12 pts win-rate, 19 metros, eight months. +$72M revenue run-rate by month nine.
Distributor base was consolidated by 30% rather than expanded — and the surviving partners delivered higher gross margin to Sentral. The five direct-sales cities are now the highest-growth metros in the portfolio.
