12  ·  Engagement Model
Cadence, decision rights, escalation — agreed before week one.
A 14-week engagement breaks because governance is left for "later." We name owners, cadence, and escalation paths before the SOW is signed.
Cadence
Tuesday SteerCo. Friday written update. Bi-weekly board readout.
Tuesday 09:00–11:00. Lead partner in the room, every week, in person in Frankfurt. No exceptions through wk 14.

Friday 17:00. One-page written update — gates progressed, blockers escalated, decisions needed next Tuesday.

Bi-weekly. 30-minute CEO + Lead Partner readout. Two pages, no PowerPoint.
Decision Rights
Halsey decides. We frame three options at every gate.
SteerCo (decides): CFO M. Hartmann (chair), CCO S. Larsson, COO J. Whitfield.

Sponsor (final escalation): CEO K. Schäfer.

Our role: at every decision gate we put three options on the table, name the trade-offs, recommend one, and stay out of the vote. No "consultants made the call" cover.
Escalation Paths
Three levels, named, with 24-hour and 72-hour clocks.
L1 — 24-hour clock. Engagement Lead (D. Burke) ↔ Halsey PMO. Operational blockers, data access.

L2 — 48-hour clock. Lead Partner (A. Vasquez) ↔ CFO M. Hartmann. Scope, gate decisions.

L3 — 72-hour clock. Managing Partner (D. Aronoff) ↔ CEO K. Schäfer. Commercial or relationship escalation.
