Our Work

Outcome
1.6× valuation uplift versus the broker pre-engagement range. All three quick wins landed pre-listing.
Anchor book covered before the second roadshow day. Atlas's H1 numbers landed at the upper half of the underwritten range, validating the model the bankers had used.
