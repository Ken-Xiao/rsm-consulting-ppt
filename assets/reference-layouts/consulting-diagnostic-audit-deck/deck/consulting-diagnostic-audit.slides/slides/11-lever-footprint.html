Lease & absorption window
Indianapolis lease expires Dec 2026 · Tulsa lease expires Mar 2027. Memphis has headroom to 71% utilization without capex. Capital-light play.
Mechanism
a · Network re-cut. Re-allocate Indianapolis (38% util.) volume to Memphis; Tulsa (32%) split Memphis / Atlanta.
b · Lease run-off. Time closures to lease expirations — no buy-out cost.
c · Labor transition. 132 FTEs across Indy + Tulsa. ~38 transferable to Memphis; balance via voluntary separation.
d · Service-level guard-rails. Re-route only if next-day-air SLA stays ≥97%.
