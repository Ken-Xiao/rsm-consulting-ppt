Exhibit 2 · Executive Answer (Minto: Situation–Complication–Resolution)
Meridian can recover $42.2M of annualized EBITDA in 18 months — the gap is not market, it is internal pricing discipline and indirect spend.
Situation
$1.18B revenue, 11.4% EBITDA margin, 4-year EBITDA flat despite 9% revenue growth.
Meridian operates 11 plants and 6 DCs across NA, supplies Tier-1 OEMs in HVAC and industrial machinery. Volume growth has come at −180 bps of gross margin and +220 bps of SG&A; cash conversion has fallen from 78% to 61%.
Complication
Performance gaps are internal — and concentrated in 5 levers.
Vs. industrial benchmark: indirect spend −420 bps, pricing leakage −180 bps, span-of-control 5.2 vs 7.0, DSO 62 vs 48 days, 2 sub-scale DCs at <40% utilization. Root cause: pricing exceptions default to CEO (RAPID broken).
Resolution
Launch a 26-week Phase-2 with 5 named workstreams and Results Delivery® governance.
Top-5 initiatives deliver $42.2M annualized; $8.4M bankable in 90 days. Phase-2 fee envelope $7.9M → 5.3× benefit-to-cost.
