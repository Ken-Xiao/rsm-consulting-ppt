Mechanism

Step 2. Position-by-position review (n=372 managers); 41 positions eliminated via attrition + selective exits.
Step 3. Manager toolkit (delegation matrix, decision rights, 1:1 rituals) so larger spans are sustainable.
Step 4. Spans reported in the standing CEO scorecard; no manager re-enters <5 span without CFO sign-off.
