SO WHAT
Two segments are fragmented enough to enter — but only acquisition makes us a credible #2 inside 18 months.
Hydraulics + modular automation are the entry beach-heads — incumbents are fragmented (top-3 < 50%) and Hartwig already wins 55–62% of head-to-head deals where invited.

Food-grade is the consolidation prize. Top-3 = 71% share and Hartwig's 29% win rate against JBT shows organic entry will not work. Acquiring Distributor B (which carries 18% of the food-grade segment) lifts Hartwig to a credible #3 from Day 1.

The three priority segments combined put Hartwig at ~8% NA share by FY29 — exactly the planning target on slide 05.
