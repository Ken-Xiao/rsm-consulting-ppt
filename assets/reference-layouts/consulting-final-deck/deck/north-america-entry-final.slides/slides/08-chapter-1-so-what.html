Three concluding bullets
01
The opportunity exists. $24B TAM growing 11% per year, with 76% of growth in three named segments. This is not a "wait and see" market — peers grew NA EBITDA 11% in 2024 while Hartwig held flat.
02
Hartwig's product fits the demand. 82% of US buyers describe the same unmet needs we already solve in Europe; only one need (domestic content) requires US-side investment.
03
The competitive structure permits entry. Hydraulics and automation segments are fragmented (top-3 < 50%); food-grade is concentrated and is the consolidation prize — accessible only by acquisition.
CONCLUSION OF CHAPTER 1
The North American opportunity is real, sized, and reachable.
The remaining question is no longer whether Hartwig should enter — but by what path.
