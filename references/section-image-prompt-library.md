# Section Image Prompt Library

用于快速生成不同金融咨询场景下的章节页背景图。所有 prompt 都应遵守 `professional-image-rulebook.md` 和 `section-divider-image-protocol.md`。

## Insurance Results

> Generate a realistic wide 16:9 professional corporate photograph of a modern financial data analysis workspace with subtle dashboard screens, blue glass architecture reflections, clean accounting advisory mood, cool blue tone, no logos, no readable text, no people close-up, no warm orange lighting, suitable for a PowerPoint section divider.

## Non-Performing Asset Practice Sharing

> Generate a realistic wide 16:9 photograph-style image of a modern blue glass financial office building and city reflection, professional banking and insurance non-performing asset resolution mood, cool blue tone, clean corporate lighting, no brand logos, no readable text, no people close-up, with open space for white geometric overlays and a bright blue vertical accent.

## Regulatory Policy / Compliance

> Generate a realistic wide 16:9 professional image of a financial regulatory review environment, clean meeting room with documents and abstract city skyline through blue glass, cool blue and white palette, no logos, no readable text, no people close-up, suitable for a consulting report section divider.

## Asset Valuation / Real Estate Finance

> Generate a realistic wide 16:9 professional image of modern commercial buildings and asset management workspace, blue glass facade, subtle financial analysis screens, cool blue corporate tone, no logos, no readable text, no people close-up, suitable for a financial consulting PowerPoint chapter divider.

## Due Diligence / Risk Review

> Generate a realistic wide 16:9 professional image of a structured due diligence review setting with organized documents, financial charts blurred in the background, blue-gray corporate lighting, no logos, no readable text, no people close-up, clean consulting style.

## Usage Notes

- Prompt 生成的是章节/封面背景，不是证据。
- 生成后必须写入 `image_assets.json`。
- 如果图片出现文字、logo、人物特写或暖色广告感，重新生成。
